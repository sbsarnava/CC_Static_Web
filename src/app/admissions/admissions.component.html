Admission Criteria

We seek individuals with a passion for technology, a strong academic foundation, and a desire to make a positive impact in the world. Admission to our Computer Science programs is competitive, and we consider factors such as academic performance, standardized test scores, letters of recommendation, and personal statements.

Application Process

Ready to apply? Follow these steps to join our vibrant community:

  1. Review the admission requirements for your desired program.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit your transcripts, standardized test scores, letters of recommendation, and any other required documents.
  4. Craft a compelling personal statement that highlights your passion for computer science and your academic and professional goals.
  5. Pay the application fee.
  6. Monitor your application status through our online portal.
